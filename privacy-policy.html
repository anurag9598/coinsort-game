Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Coin Sort Puzzle ("the App") is developed by Coin Sort Studios. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect (spoiler: none) and how we handle your information when you use our App.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever.

The App:

  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or storage
  • Does not use advertising SDKs or analytics services
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Local Storage

The App may save your highest score and current level progress locally on your device using your device's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is only used to resume your game session.

Permissions

The App requests no special Android permissions. It does not access:

  • Internet (no network calls)
  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Location services
  • External storage

Children's Privacy

The App is rated Everyone (E) and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 or any age group, as we collect no data at all.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or social media. There are no in-app purchases or external links.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
